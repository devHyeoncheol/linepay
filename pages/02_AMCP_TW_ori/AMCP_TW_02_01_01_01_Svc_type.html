What is a QR scan code?

Payment will be made when the payment QR code is placed in the store and the customer scans the smartphone, without the need to install additional equipment or introduce a dedicated application. If the review is passed after the application, it can be used immediately as App POS.

* When you apply for LINE Pay, there is a review. Failure to comply with the screening criteria may result in a failure to pass.

Fixed amount

What is a LINE Pay mini?

After installing the store, turn on the power, enter the amount only, the payment is complete! When a customer scans, they will be charged immediately, without having to enter a payment amount.

* When you apply for LINE Pay merchant, you will be judged. Failure to meet the screening criteria may result in failure.
* After you apply for membership, you will be sent from LINE Pay when the merchant examination is completed.

What is a LINE Pay Golchi terminal?

  1. Step 1

    Enter amount by member store owner

  2. Step 2

    The amount entered appears on the terminal as QR code and number for payment.

  3. Step 3

    The customer scans the QR code displayed on the stationary terminal and completes the payment.
