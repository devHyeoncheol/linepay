Merchant type
Required documents
Corporation
  • Certified copy of the certificate of registration、Incorporation issued by government authorities、Certificate of business tax status (alternative)
  • The latest copy of the owner’s ID card
  • Business account bankbook (the beneficiary must same as the company registration name)
  • For the physical store, it's necessary to attach the storefront photo and the product DM which include price
  • For the online store, it’s necessary to provide the official website (must be able to purchase)
  • For prepaid type product must provide the performance bond related documents
Individual
  • The latest copy of the owner’s ID card
  • Personal account bankbook (the beneficiary must same as the owner’s name)
  • Business account bankbook (the beneficiary must same as the company registration name)
  • For the physical store, it's necessary to attach the storefront photo and the product DM which include price

* Not open following industry apply individual merchant: Online store/Premium price product(unit price higher than ten thousand)/Prepaid type product/Hotel and Guesthouse, etc
