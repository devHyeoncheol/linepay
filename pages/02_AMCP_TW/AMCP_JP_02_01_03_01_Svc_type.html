What is a QR scan code?

Payment will be made when the payment QR code is placed in the store and the customer scans the smartphone, without the need to install additional equipment or introduce a dedicated application. If the review is passed after the application, it can be used immediately as App POS.

* When you apply for LINE Pay, there is a review. Failure to comply with the screening criteria may result in a failure to pass.

What is a non-stop terminal?

Install it in the store, power it on, and enter the amount to complete the payment! If the customer scans, the payment will be made immediately without having to enter the payment amount.

* When you apply for LINE Pay, there is a review. Failure to comply with the screening criteria may result in a failure to pass.

  • Settlement account

    Settlement accounts have two options.

    LINE Pay Balance
    If you are a member of LINE Pay Money, you will receive the settlement amount with Balance.
    Bank Account
    Receive the settlement amount to the merchant bank account you set up.

    The payment account information can be changed through the merchant center > Basic information management > Deposit account management > Change account menu.

  • Settlement Cycle

    The settlement takes place on the 19th of each month, and the outstanding transactions are remitted to the bank account at the end of next month. (If the money was processed in March, the money will be transferred at the end of April)

    A fee of 240 yen (tax included) per deposit is charged.

    Application for settlement will be made at the time of application for deposit or once a month (the end of the following day). Depending on the type of bank account you're using, there may be a delay.
